The Sports Offered at Elite Courts

Padel Courts

Play on our state-of-the-art **panoramic glass courts** with WPT-certified blue turf. Enjoy perfect visibility and consistent bounce for competitive play.

Pickleball Setup

Dedicated, standard-sized **Pickleball courts** with high-quality netting and non-slip surfaces. Perfect for singles and doubles matches.

Cricket Nets

High-quality, floodlight **cricket practice lane** pitching machine with different balling options (Swing, spin, or pace (upto 160km/h).

Shared Elite Amenities

All users benefit from modern changing rooms, high-speed Wi-Fi, clean bathrooms, ample free parking, and a coffeeshop stocked with various drinks and snacks.
